UUMALI ni nini?
UMALI ni Umoja wa Utunzaji wa Msitu wa Asili Lijala, unaolenga kuhifadhi mazingira na kukuza maendeleo endelevu kwa jamii ya Lindi.

Kwa nini UUMALI ilianzishwa?
Ili kukabiliana na uharibifu wa mazingira na misitu ya asili katika eneo la Lijala na maeneo jirani.

Shughuli za UUMALI ni zipi?
- Uhifadhi wa misitu
- Elimu kwa jamii
- Upandaji miti na ufugaji nyuki
- Utafiti wa mazingira

Ninawezaje kushiriki?
Kwa kujiunga kama mwanachama, kujitolea au kushirikiana kwenye miradi ya kimazingira.

UUMALI iko wapi?
Mnazimmoja, Wilaya ya Lindi, Mkoa wa Lindi, Tanzania.

Mafanikio yetu ni yapi?
Tumefanikisha upandaji miti 3,000+, semina 10+ za kijamii, na utunzaji wa hekta 50 za msitu wa asili.

ENGLISH

What is UUMALI?
UUMALI is the Community Forest Conservation Association of Lijala, focused on environmental protection and sustainable development in Lindi.

Why was UUMALI established?
To combat forest degradation and environmental damage in Lijala and nearby areas.

What does UUMALI do?
- Forest conservation
- Community education
- Tree planting and beekeeping
- Environmental research

How can I participate?
By becoming a member, volunteering, or partnering on green projects.

Where is UUMALI located?
Mnazimmoja, Lindi District, Lindi Region, Tanzania.

What are our achievements?
Planted over 3,000 trees, held 10+ community seminars, and protected 50 hectares of forest.
