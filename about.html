KISWAHILI

UUMALI ni nini?
Ni Ufupisho maneno haya, Ufugaji wa Nyuki na Utunzaji wa msitu wa asili lijala (UUMALI). Shirika hili sio la kiserikali na lilianzishwa kwa nia ya kuongeza nguvu katika mapambano dhidi ya uharibifu wa misitu asili na mazingira kwa ujumla miongoni mwa jamii zetu na kushawishi wanajamii kutoharibu mazingira na kuhimiza matumizi ya nishati jadilifu ili kupunguza madhara yatokanayo na uharibifu wa misitu. Kwa kuona ongezeko la uharibifu wa misitu, ukataji miti ovyo, na kupotea kwa viumbe hai, tuliona ni muhimu kuanzisha UMALI kama suluhisho la kudumu kwa kizazi cha sasa na vijavyo. Kupitia ushirikiano na jamii, tunahamasisha uhifadhi, tunatoa elimu ya mazingira, na kuhimiza uchumi wa kijani kupitia ufugaji wa nyuki na upandaji miti.

Kwa nini UUMALI ilianzishwa?
Shirika hili lilianzishwa ili kukabiliana na changamoto za uharibifu wa mazingira, ukataji miti, na kutokuwepo kwa ajira endelevu vijijini.

Shughuli za UUMALI ni zipi?
- Utunzaji wa Msitu wa Asili Lijala
- Ufugaji wa nyuki
- Elimu ya mazingira kwa jamii
- Ushirikiano na taasisi zingine

Ninawezaje kushiriki?
Unaweza kujiunga kama mwanachama, kujitolea, au kutoa msaada wa vifaa, fedha au utaalamu.

UUMALI iko wapi?
Makao makuu yapo Mnazimmoja, Lindi. Inasimamiwa na Kamati ya Utendaji ya watu wa jamii.

Mafanikio yetu ni yapi?
Tumefaulu kuhifadhi msitu, kufundisha jamii kuhusu mazingira, na kuanzisha miradi ya asali.

Wasiliana nasi
Tumefunguka kwa mawasiliano: Simu, barua pepe au tembelea ofisi yetu.

ENGLISH

What is UUMALI?
UMALI is a non-governmental organization dedicated to conserving natural forests, protecting the environment, and promoting beekeeping for sustainable community development in Mnazimmoja, Lindi, Tanzania.

Why was UUMALI established?
The organization was created to address the challenges of environmental degradation, deforestation, and lack of sustainable rural employment.

What does UUMALI do?
- Conserving Lijala Natural Forest
- Promoting beekeeping
- Community environmental education
- Partnering with other institutions

How can I participate?
You can join as a member, volunteer, or support with equipment, funds, or expertise.

Where is UUMALI located?
Headquarters are in Mnazimmoja, Lindi. The organization is managed by a Community Executive Committee.

What are our achievements?
We have successfully preserved forest areas, educated the community, and launched honey production projects.

Contact Us
We are open for communication: Call us, send an email, or visit our office.
